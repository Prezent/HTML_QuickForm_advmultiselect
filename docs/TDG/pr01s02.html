Pre-requisites

It is assumed that you have installed both PHP and PEAR, and that you have rudimentary knowledge of PHP, HTML and JavaScript.

It is also assumed that your PHP scripts are correctly interpreted by your web server. In most case it will be probably Apache, but you may also have IIS. HTML_QuickForm_advmultiselect runs both on Windows and *nix.
